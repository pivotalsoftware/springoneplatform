From San Francisco International Airport (SFO)

The easiest way to get to Moscone Center West from SFO is via BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit rail system). BART trains depart from the International Terminal every 15 minutes and it takes around 30 minutes to downtown San Francisco. If you arrive at the domestic terminals, take the AirTrain to get to the BART station which is located at the International Terminal. A one-way ticket from SFO to Powell Street Station in downtown San Francisco will cost approximately $8.95. BART tickets can be purchased using cash or credit card at the vending machines in the BART station. Take the "Pittsburg/Bay Point" train and disembark at the Powell Street station. Exit the station onto Market Street and walk southwest toward 4th Street. Turn left onto 4th street, then left onto Howard Street.

From Oakland International Airport (OAK)

The easiest way to get to Moscone Center from OAK is via BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit rail system). BART trains depart across from the Terminal 1 baggage claim area, and a short walk from Terminal 2. A one-way ticket to Powell Street Station in downtown San Francisco will cost approximately $3.75. BART tickets can be purchased using cash or credit at the vending machines in the BART station. Take the “Coliseum” train and get off at the “Coliseum” station. You will then need to transfer onto the “Daly City” train which will bring you into Downtown San Francisco. Disembark at the Powell Street station, and exit onto Market Street. Walk southwest toward 4th Street. Turn left onto 4th Street, then left onto Howard Street.


More information on BART: https://www.bart.gov/guide

Uber and Lyft are also available from both SFO and OAK airports.

If you arrive at SFO, Uber and Lyft pick-up from the Departures Level at each terminal. If you have checked baggage, you will need to return upstairs to the Departures Level once you’ve retrieved your baggage. You’ll be required to provide both your Terminal and Door number when requesting your ride. This information is located on every door when exiting the airport.


If you arrive at OAK, Uber and Lyft pick-up from the Arrivals Level at each terminal.
