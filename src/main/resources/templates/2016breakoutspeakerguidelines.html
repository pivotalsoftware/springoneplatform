You must turn in the source file for your presentation immediately before or immediately after delivering your presentation - no exceptions. Technical Requirements

Generally we’re assuming that your presentation will be run from your personal laptop. Most modern laptops with common presentation software tend to connect smoothly to the projectors. Laptops are connected to an external recording system, no screen capture software is used. Machines are placed on the onstage podium; you’ll use your laptop’s display for reference while speaking. We discourage using embedded audio/video in your decks as this requires special accommodation to be recorded. Lavalier mics are the default equipment to ensure good audio capture.

Presentation Specifications

  • You are asked to use our minimal, themed template for your presentation (PPT, Keynote and ODP provided). There are also many related graphics, color palettes, and logos you might find useful. If you use something other than those programs, please ensure that you can control both aspect ratio and resolution setting so they meet specs below.
  • Presentation Specs:
    • 16:9 aspect ratio
    • 1280x720 is the target (But perfect 16:9 ratios may work up to 1920x1080)
    • Bring your network dongle for hard lines at podiums
    • We’ll communicate shortly about laptop to projector dongles
  • Contact Patrick Gillis if you need help with this at pgillis@pivotal.io.

Code of Conduct

Our event requires everyone, including speakers, to follow the code of conduct so we can make the “conference a place that is welcoming and respectful to all participants”. Specifically, “sexual or racist comments or images in slides are not appropriate”. We require you to ensure your presentation materials and talk track are in accordance with this.
