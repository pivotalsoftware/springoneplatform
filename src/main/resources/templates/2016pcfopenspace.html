Open Space

Open Space is the simplest meeting that could possibly work, based on (un)common sense of what people do naturally in productive meetings.

You are invited to join with all Pivotal Cloud Foundry users for a community discussion about the future, August 1st from 1:00–5:00 pm at SpringOne Platform in Bristlecone 5.

We’ll spend half a day discussing everything Pivotal Cloud Foundry: operations, development, tips, tricks, roadmap and anything else that the group is interested in exploring. Regardless of where you are on the journey, from Cloud Native curious to Cloud Foundry in production, you’ll be joining the Pivotal Cloud Foundry experts and users in unfiltered discussion about what works and where we all go from here.
