Pre-Conference Training at SpringOne Platform

December 3–4, 2017

Pivotal Training offers a series of hands-on, technical courses prior to SpringOne Platform. Classes are scheduled two full days before the conference and provide you and your team an opportunity to receive in-depth, lab-based training across some of the latest Pivotal technologies.



All pre-conference classes are taught by Pivotal Certified Instructors and are available exclusively to SpringOne Platform attendees. Best of all, classes cost a fraction of the price compared to traditional instructor-led training and do not overlap with the main conference sessions.

What's in it for me

  • Hands-on, interactive training delivered at accelerated rate and lower cost
  • Lab-oriented content and expertly designed course materials that helps maximize skills retention and increase proficiency
  • Opportunity to network with Pivotal Certified Instructors and technology enthusiasts
  • Ability to save valuable time and money by adding training as part of your existing registration



Logistics

When

  • December 3rd and 4th (all classes are two full days but do not overlap with conference activities)
  • All courses run from 9 a.m.–5 p.m. both days

Expectations

  • All courses include hands-on labs and require attendees to provide their own laptop
  • Laptops should include all necessary hardware and software already installed; A list of “technical requirements” will be provided prior to class

Pricing & Registration

  • Training workshops start at $600 per student (increasing to $650 and then $700 as shown during conference registration)
  • Your preferred workshop can be selected during registration for SpringOne Platform. Space is limited.

Please email the education team with any questions.
