Presenter Support

  • Demo table - as needed
  • Slide deck wireless eueing system (slide advancement)
  • Internet access
  • Water bottles
  • Four (4) 55” downstage monitors displaying slide deck content (Current/Current or Current/Notes)
  • (2) Digital display speaker timers

Content Screen Size

  • (2 screens) The screens are in 3:1 aspect ratio measuring 17’x51’, but we will PIP 16:9 aspect ratio content slides which will be 17’x30’.

Video Dimensions and Format

Created in 16:9 aspect ratio – The preferred format for content production is Apple ProRes HD or Apple ProRes H.264. @ 1920x1080, 29.97fps.

Audio Support

All presenters will have access to a wireless microphone and be pre-mic’d and sound checked backstage prior to starting. Additional hand held microphones are available for any Q&A and back up as necessary.

Lighting Support

The Main Stage will be complemented with a complete theatrical lighting system including color changing ability allowing for custom lighting effects for walk-in, presentations and transitions. It is anticipated that each presenter can have a distinct lighting environment.

Video Support

The Main Stage screens are supported by a complete state of the art projection, switching, playback and record system with redundant equipment throughout. The video switching system can switch between multiple sources including:

  • Speaker Support Graphics Computer
  • Video Playback System (please refer to video dimensions and format above)
  • Internet Connections - direct connect as requested

In addition to switching between multiple sources for presentations and demos, there are two (3) production cameras in the room that could also be switched to the screens and provide Image Magnification of the speakers on stage (I-Mag).

Recommended Attire

  • Button down shirt is best to clip microphones onto; no crew neck t-shirts
  • Avoid white or striped shirts
  • No loose necklaces and/or jewelry which may interfere with a lapel clip mic
  • Pants are recommended for connecting and clipping your wireless microphone receiver
  • If a dress is worn, please wear slim belt under the dress to clip on the microphone receiver
  • Please remove lanyard name badge before your stage presentation
  • Turn off personal mobile devices prior to your stage presentation
